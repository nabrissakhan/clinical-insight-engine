Clinical Insight Pipeline — System Architecture
================================================

Input: FHIR R4 Patient Bundle (JSON)
        ↓
load_patient_bundle()
        ↓
retrieve_patient_facts()
    - Extract patient demographics
    - Extract conditions
    - Extract medications
    - Extract observations (labs)
    - Extract diagnostic reports
        ↓
generate_clinical_insight()
    - Convert structured facts into readable summary
    - Include safety disclaimer
        ↓
validate_clinical_output()
    - Check for unsafe medical advice language
    - Ensure disclaimer is present
    - Ensure synthetic data disclosure is present
    - Return pass/fail + issues list
        ↓
evaluate_confidence()
    - Score based on data completeness (0.0 - 1.0)
    - Adjust based on insight output length
    - Assign confidence label (High / Moderate / Low)
        ↓
Decision
    ├── Validation passed → Display clinical insight
    └── Validation failed → Display guardrail violations
